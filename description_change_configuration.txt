The client accesses the webapp (Self-Service CRM) 
where he can: add/remove an IoT device from the
network, change an iot device or service onfigurations or leave the webapp. 
Requesting to add/remove a IoT device from the network will start the 
add_remove_iot_device process.
Requesting to change a device/service configuration will result on the 
configuration parameters being updated on a database.
